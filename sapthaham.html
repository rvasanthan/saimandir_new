Shri Sai Satcharitra Saptaham - Parayanam

On the occasion of Sankranthi/Pongal, Shirdi Sai Mandir Maryland (Saimandir.org) is planning for Shri Sai Satcharitra Saptaham (Parayanam in 7 days) for this 1st quarter (soon after Sankranthi week) of 2023. Parayanam will start from Friday (01/27/2023) and end on Thursday (02/02/2023) Baba Parayanam timings will be evening 6:30pm to 8:30pm (from Friday to Thursday) and on Thursday (02/02/2023) 5:30pm to 7:30pm followed by and Shej Arthi. Those who are interested can register for one chapter per person and the rest of the chapters we can listen while other devotees are reading everyday.

All 51 chapters from Shri Sai Satcharitra will be read in 7 days by as many devotees as possible to introduce Sai Tatvam (the way of happy living) in man kind.

  • Friday: 1 to 7 chapters
  • Saturday: 8 to 15 chapters
  • Sunday: 16 to 22 chapters
  • Monday: 23 to 30 chapters
  • Tuesday: 31 to 37 chapters
  • Wednesday: 38 to 44 chapters
  • Thursday: 45 to 51 chapters

About Shirdi Sai Mandir

Our Shirdi Sai Mandir of Maryland is a non profit organization established in 2018 by our trustees. Our main goal is to spread the faith, happiness and spiritualty in Howard County area. Working towards this vision and with the help of our members with ideal and noble motives, we are moving forward with a Mandir in Columbia, Maryland.
