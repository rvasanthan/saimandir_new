Temple Visiting & Guidelines



Sri Shirdi Sai Mandir of Maryland takes immense pleasure to invite all our devotees to visit our temple .Our temple is open every day from 9 AM to 8 PM EST. We recommend all our devotees to call to +1 443 636 7807 if you are planning to visit the temple. Please find the Guidelines for our devotees to visit our temple

  • Please wear a face mask while entering the temple premises.
  • Please maintain Social distancing.
  • Always keep distance from the priest and temple volunteers
  • Follow the directions provided by the volunteers and priest to maintain the sanity and safety of everyone.
  • Please refrain from visiting the temple if you have any symptoms or exposed to covid within the past 10 days.
