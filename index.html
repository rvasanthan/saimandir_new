Our Sai Mandir

The First ever Sai temple to be built in Howard County of Maryland

Look out into the universe and contemplate the glory of Saibaba. Observe the stars, millions of them, twinkling in the night sky, all with a message of unity, part of the very nature of Shirdi baba.

Our Shirdi Sai Mandir of Maryland is a non profit organization established in 2018 by our trustees. Our main goal is to spread the faith, happiness and spiritualty in Howard County area. Working towards this vision and with the help of our members with ideal and noble motives, we are moving forward with a temple in Howard County, Maryland.

Our Services

Puja and Aarthi

  • Date: Every Thursday 7.00PM - 9.00PM

We have aarthi will be done on every thursday evening in our Sai Mandir. Our Aarathis are given to baba between 7.30 PM to 9 PM in the evening. Currently, we are running in a temporary space and soon we will have a dedicated place for our temple. So, we are having the puja, bhajan and Aarathi only on thursday evenings. However, we will have celebrations and special pujas on auspicious days like Sankranthi, New Year, Ugadhi and more traditional festivals.

Sponsorship and Patronage

As a respected Sai Devotee, You have several opportunities in raising our Sai Mandir in various ways. You can sponsor flowers for Baba, Food for Baba and our devotees and any help in properous development of our place which is being established for Baba. Also, Once we get a permament place for Baba, We have many plans to serve lot of non religious community activites.

Volunteering

Our Sai Mandir is new and we have many good hearted young individuals who are helping us in all possible ways. We are welcoming more sai devotees to join hands with us. We will be engaged in a lot of activities to serve the communities once we move to the permemant place of Sai Mandir, where we need a lot of helping hands from all your good hearts.
