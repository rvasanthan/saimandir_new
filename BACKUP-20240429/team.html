Our Pradhana Archaka

Sri Manjunathaiah Hiremathada is our respected Pradhana Archaka of our Sri Shirdi Sai Mandir Ellicott City Maryland. Manjunath Ji has been a resident of the United States of America for more than 10 years. As a very well known priest in the greater Baltimore area, he is serving us from December 28, 2020. For any inquiries regarding poojas, performed either at the Mandir or your home, please contact us via email at seva@saimandir.org or make a call to +1 240 779 6591. For more details visit SaiMandir.org.

About Shirdi Sai Mandir

Our Shirdi Sai Mandir of Maryland is a non profit organization established in 2018 by our trustees. Our main goal is to spread the faith, happiness and spiritualty in Howard County area. Working towards this vision and with the help of our members with ideal and noble motives, we are moving forward with a Mandir in Columbia, Maryland.
