Look out into the universe and contemplate the glory of Saibaba. Observe the stars, millions of them, twinkling in the night sky, all with a message of unity, part of the very nature of Shirdi baba.

Volunteering Services at Sai Mandir


Embrace the spirit of service and become a vital part of our mandir community. At Shridi Sai Mandir, we recognize the significance of youth in shaping the future and carrying forward the principles that define our sacred space. As a token of our appreciation, we proudly present the President's Volunteer Service Award to individuals who dedicate their time and efforts to help our mandir thrive (to learn more presidentialserviceawards.gov). We celebrate the achievements of our community members, and invite you to join this legacy of service. Through the upcoming year, Shridi Sai Mandir will be creating ample opportunities for community members to get involved. Anyone who is 13 years and older are welcome to participate and If you have any additional questions please feel free to reach us at volunteer@saimandir.org. Looking forward to making a difference in the world with Sai Baba's teaching with all of you.
