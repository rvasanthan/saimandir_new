About Shirdi Sai Mandir

Our Shirdi Sai Mandir of Maryland is a non profit organization established in 2018 by our trustees. Our main goal is to spread the faith, happiness and spiritualty in Howard County area. Working towards this vision and with the help of our members with ideal and noble motives, we are moving forward with a temple in Columbia, Maryland.
